## About me

Policy & Data Associate for Portfolio Quality at the [Asian Infrastructure Investment Bank (AIIB)](https://www.aiib.org/) in Beijing. My work spans data analytics, portfolio monitoring, and evidence-based policy, primarily in the context of infrastructure investment in Asia.

Previously I was a Data Scientist at the [Carbon Trust](https://www.carbontrust.com/), where I spent most of my time building and validating Environmentally Extended Input Output (EEIO) models and developing spend-based emission factor methodologies for scope 3 GHG accounting. Before that I was an Economic Modeller at [Cambridge Econometrics](https://www.camecon.com/), working on macroeconomic modelling (E3ME), energy systems, and data-intensive research projects for clients including the European Commission, IRENA, and the Rockwool Foundation.

My research interests sit at the intersection of environmental economics, urban and spatial policy, and applied modelling. I work primarily in Python, R, and Julia.
