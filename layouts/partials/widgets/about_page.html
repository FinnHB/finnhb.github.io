About me

Hello!

I’m an economist by training, who is curious about how fundamental interactions shape the world around us, especially at the cross-roads between the finance, economics, and the environment. I sometimes post on the blog things I find interesting. Although I mostly post about things related to modelling, economics, and the environment, a few other topics may occasionally slip through!

Currently based in Beijing, I am coordinating AIIB’s ESG efforts and support the environmental and social policy assurance for Bank-financed projects. Before joining AIIB, I focused on modelling relationships between the environment and macroeconomy. First, through scenario analysis for multilateral institutions at Cambridge Econometrics, and then by developing bespoke environmental-economic models for estimating supply chain emissions (scope 3) at Carbon Trust.

Feel free to reach out on LinkedIn or drop me a line at finn_henrik@hotmail.com. If you're ever curious about any of the code, you can find it on github, I try my best to keep it well documented.
